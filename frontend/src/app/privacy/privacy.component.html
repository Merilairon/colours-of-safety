Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

Colours of Safety ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our community map platform for LGBTQIA+ safe spaces.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address (required for account creation)
  • Display name (shown publicly with your submissions)
  • Password (stored hashed and salted)

2.2 Submissions

  • Place and district names, descriptions, and locations
  • Safety ratings and categories
  • Timestamps of submissions and reviews

2.3 Technical Information

  • IP address (for rate limiting and security)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the service
  • To display your contributions on the map (display name only)
  • To communicate about your submissions (approval/rejection status)
  • To prevent spam and abuse
  • To improve the platform

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent

Your email address is never displayed publicly. Only your display name is shown with your submissions.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security reviews and updates

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@coloursofsafety.com

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

  • Authentication token: Stores your login session
  • User preferences: Remembers your display settings

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained until you delete your account
  • Submissions: retained indefinitely as they are part of the community map
  • IP logs: retained for 30 days for security purposes

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform.

10. Open Data Licence

The community-contributed map data on this platform (place names, district information, safety ratings, descriptions, and geographic locations) is made available under the Open Database Licence (ODbL) v1.0.

Under the ODbL you are free to:

  • Share — copy and redistribute the database
  • Create — produce works from the database
  • Adapt — modify, transform, and build upon the database

Provided that you:

  • Attribute Colours of Safety as the source
  • Share any adapted database under the same ODbL licence
  • Keep the database open if you redistribute it

Personal data (account information, email addresses) is not covered by this licence and remains subject to the terms above.

Attribution: "Map data © Colours of Safety contributors, available under ODbL."
